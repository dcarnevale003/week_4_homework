Fantastic Four Vs. Doctor Doom

Rules of the Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are the four members of the Fantastic Four below. By clicking on one of the Fantastic Four youl will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

If you match your total score to the random number Doctor Doom meets his demise, but if your total score goes over the random number Doctor Doom will emerge as victorious.

The value of each Fantastic Four member is hidden from you until you click on them.

Each time the game starts the game will change the value of each Fantastic Four member.

The fate of the Fantastic Four lies in your hands!
